Our Mission

At OTT Release Pro, we are passionate about movies. With the rapid explosion of OTT platforms—Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar, and many others—it can be overwhelming to keep track of where and when a movie is releasing. Our mission is to simplify this process and bring all tracking data into one seamless, fast, and highly intuitive platform.

What We Offer

  • Real-time Updates: Get instant updates on trending and upcoming movie releases.
  • Dynamic Search: Easily find your favorite movies with our dynamic TMDB-powered search engine.
  • Filter Capabilities: Discover movies exactly tailored to your taste by filtering through Language, Genre, and Release Year.
  • Trailer Integration: Watch YouTube trailers instantly via our seamless popup modals without ever leaving the tracking interface.
